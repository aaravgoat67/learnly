Learnly is a free AI-powered study platform built for school students. Connect with your school, track homework and exam results, share notes with classmates, and chat in study groups — all in one place. Features:

🏫 Join your school with an invite code 📋 Track homework with due date reminders 📊 Log exam results and get AI study advice 📝 Share notes with your whole school 🎯 Generate quizzes from your notes using AI 💬 Real-time study group chat with an AI helper 🏆 Student leaderboard to stay motivated
